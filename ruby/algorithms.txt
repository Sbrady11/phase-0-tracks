Elevator robot pseudo-code

-Record current floor position.
-If desired floor is above your position, press 'up'
	-Else, press 'down'.
-Enter elevator once doors open.
-select desired floor.
-press the button.
-until elevator stops, remain in elevator.
-exit once the doors open.

Making Coffee

-Fill canister with water
-Open pod hatch
	-Insert desired coffee pod
-Close pod hatch
-Place mug underneath machine faucet
-On the touch screen, select desired coffee size, and select 'brew'
	-do not remove cup until the coffee finishes dispensing
-wait until cool
	-then drink
